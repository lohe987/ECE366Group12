
**Instruction Set Architecture for the Limited Instruction Set**

ADD 100 xx, yy
		Rx  Ry

LWD 01 xx, yyy
	   Rx  constant
	   (Rx can only be r2 or r3)

SWD 00 xx, yyy
	   Rx, constant
	   (Rx can only be r0)
	   (constant cannot be 0)

SLE 110 xx, yy
	    Rx  Ry

XOR	101 xx, yy
		Rx  Ry

SHL 111 xx, yy
		Rx  Const

JIF 010 xxxx
		constant
**If r3 == 1 jump (PC = PC + constant)**
**else PC = PC + 1**

ADDI 0001 x, yy
		 Rx  constant
	(Rx must be r0 or r1)

INIT 001 xx, yy
		 Rx, constant

HLT	000 00 00
